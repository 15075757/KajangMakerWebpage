STEM Education in Kajang

Training in the
Real World

At Kajang Maker, learning happens through doing. Our hands-on workshops put students in front of real robots, real circuits, and real AI challenges — not just textbooks. From primary school children discovering electronics to secondary students competing in national championships, we meet learners where they are.

Founded in 2015, we have grown from a single workshop space into a thriving community of curious minds, dedicated mentors, and proud school partners across Kajang and the Klang Valley.

Artificial Intelligence

AI Applies
to Everyone

AI is not just for engineers and data scientists — it is the language of the future. Kajang Maker introduces students to practical AI concepts: training simple models, understanding how recommendation systems work, and building projects that use real machine learning tools.

We make AI approachable, fun, and immediately relevant to students' everyday lives — no prior coding experience required.
